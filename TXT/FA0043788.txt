CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish.
