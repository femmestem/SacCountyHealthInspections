# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Hand wash sink located behind front counter was blocked by several bags of prepackaged chips. Maintain all hand wash sinks remain clear and accessible at all times. Corrected on site.

MAJOR

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of proof that an owner or employee has passed an approved and accredited food safety certification examination. Sign up with an approved and accredited food safety certification examination within 30 days and provide proof to EMD within 60 days by fax (916-875-8513, att., Patel) or email (patelt@saccounty.net) . Handout provided.
CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: Food handler cards not provided for all employees. Provide food handler card for every employee and maintain copy of food handler card at the facility for review. Correct within 30 days. Provided California Food Handler Card Law handout.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948) OVERALL INSPECTION COMMENTS
