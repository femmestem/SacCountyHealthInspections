# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: Facility's food safety certification is expired. Enroll in an accredited examination and re-certify within 30 days. Note: Manager has examination date scheduled for 2/23/16.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1) # 4 EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE
OBSERVATIONS: Employee's open beverage is stored on rack above an open box of tea bags. Corrected; personal beverage was relocated to office desk during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from contamination. (113977, 114256 (a)) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Counter top at the base of water heater is unclean with moderate dust debris build-up. Corrected on-site.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
