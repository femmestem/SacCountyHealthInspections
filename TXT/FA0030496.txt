# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Measured a pan of shredded cheese (54F) inside of prep line reach in refrigeration unit located across from grill. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHFs) at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, per chef, the food item had been placed inside of reach in after having been out at room temperature for past 20 minutes.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F.
