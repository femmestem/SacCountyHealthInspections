# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: MEASURED 83F AT WAREWASH SINK. CORRECT IMMEDIATELY AND FACILITY IS PROHIBITED FROM SELLING ICE. PROVIDE PROOF OF REPAIR IN 24 HOURS.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F.
