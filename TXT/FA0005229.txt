# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Dishwasher reaches 160F at the plate. The trays come out clean with no soap or food residue. Currently the dishwasher has to manually be stopped since it doesn't do it on it's own. The soap dispenser is constantly pumping. The staff washes all dishes with soap prior to placing inside dishwasher. Then they run it in the dishwasher for a unspecified period of time and then take out the dishes for air drying. The water is still spraying when they lift the lid. Repair dishwasher so that it runs through a wash, rinse and sanitation cycle. Provide proof of correction within 2 weeks.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. # 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: High temperature dishwasher thermometer doesn't turn on. Replace battery or thermometer within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
