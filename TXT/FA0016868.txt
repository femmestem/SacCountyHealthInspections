# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Found 1 live cockroach in dry storage. 2 dead cockroach bodies found in dry storage. Found the following evidence that facility prepares ready to eat food items: cutting boards; knives; slicers; manual masher; portioned sour cream and feta cheese.
Corrective actions: 1. Facility is required to cease any/all preparation of ready to eat food. Portioning sour cream and feta cheese - cutting or slicing activities are not approved. 2. Protect food and food contact equipment from contamination. 3. Begin regular treatment and monitoring to exterminate active vermin infestation. 4. Contact EMD at (916)875-8440 to schedule re-inspection after no evidence of an active vermin infestation can be found.
Note: A re-inspection may be conducted to determine compliance. A $201 fee will be applied for each re-inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
