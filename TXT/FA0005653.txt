# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Soap not dispensing at Ovens hand sink (pizza station). Dispenser was not working, a squeeze bottle of soap was provided during inspection. Repair or replace dispenser - 2 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

MAJOR

# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: Current food handler cards not available for all employees. Correct - 15 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Shredded cheese at 50 F in ice bath in Taqueria station. Ice bath level was not equal to product in container. Provide an adequate ice bath or keep in working refrigeration. Correct today.
Single serving containers of cream cheese at 45 F in condiment bar. This item is not kept on the time log. All potentially hazardous foods must be kept using a time log, or kept at 41 F or below or 135 F or above.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 8 TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL
OBSERVATIONS: Log for self service sandwich bar did not have times written on it, only food items. All items are put out for the lunch service only (11:30 am to 1 pm), then discarded. Corrected during inspection. Note: When using time as a public health control, provide written logs with date, food item and discard time (no more than 4 hours after time it was removed from temperature control) for all potentially hazardous foods. Any food for which TPHC is used and is found not marked must be discarded per CalCode 114000(a)(3).
CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)
# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Whole fruit available for customer self service lacks sneeze guard. Provide protection for any fruit that is ready to eat (does not require peeling). Correct - 1 day.

# 28 WASHING FRUITS AND VEGETABLES
OBSERVATIONS: Fruit available for customer self service is not washed prior to being put out for service. Correct - 1 day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Produce shall be thoroughly washed in potable water to remove soil and other contaminants before being cut, combined with other ingredients, cooked, served, or offered for human consumption. (113992)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Debris on floor under Grill equipment. Clean and maintain more regularly - 1 day.
Soda syrup build up on floor under soda syrup rack in beverage room. Correct - 1 day
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Ambient temperature of prep refrigerator in Ovens station was 46 F. All food items are brought into this area for lunch service only and had been stocked immediately before inspection. Do not use this unit for any food items that are potentially hazardous and not kept using time as a public health control until unit is repaired or replaced.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)
