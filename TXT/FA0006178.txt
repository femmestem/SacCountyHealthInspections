# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Paper towels are stored on the hand sink counters for both the men and women's restrooms. Lacking towel dispensers for both restrooms. Correct within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Pink and black mold like patches on the white panel inside the ice machine. REPEAT VIOLATION. Correct within 24 hours.
Dish machine, while in use to wash a rack of glassware, measured less than 50ppm. Sanitizer liquid in the bottle was low. Staff checked and found no back up supply of sanitizer. Instructed staff to use the dish machine in the kitchen to wash the rack of glassware that was in the machine. Use the kitchen dish machine to wash glassware until the bar dish machine is resupplied with sanitizer. Correct within 24 hours. Send proof of correction to inspector by fax or email. FAX 916-875-8513 attention to M.Oetzel or email Oetzelm@saccounty.net
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Heavy food debris on floor underneath all the cook's line refrigeration units and tables. The floor surface, located underneath the cooking equipment at the hood, has food debris. Deep clean within 14 days.
Prep line meat refrigerator has accumulation of food debris on the bottom of the refrigerator, food debris caught in the creases of the door gasket. Correct within 3 days.
Single door upright freezer has accumulation of frozen fries, and frozen food debris on the bottom of the freezer. Correct within 3 days.
Wall panel behind the dish machine in the kitchen, has black mold like patches on it. Correct within 3 days.
Hand sink at the cook's line: wall panels around the hand sink have build up of food residue, also food debris on the ledge above the hand sink. Correct within 2 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 36 EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Pizza dough roller found wedged between the prep line refrigerators. Improper and unsanitary storage of utensils. REPEAT VIOLATION. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled, displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3, 114185.4, 114185.5)
# 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: Thermometer missing for the prep line meat refrigerator located at the cook's line. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
# 44d EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS
OBSERVATIONS: Emplioyee open can beverage stored on the top shelf of the single door upright freezer at the cook's line. Provide a separate area for employees to store beverages. Item removed from freezer during inspection.
Employee shirt found laying on top of food supply product on the shelf rack located at the end of the cook's line. REPEAT VIOLATION. Provide a separate area for employees to store clothing. Corrected during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)

# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: Ceiling panels missing above the ansul system box on the wall. Replace missing tiles within 14 days.
Grout is worn and has accumulation of dirt and debris at the hand sink located at the cook's line. Regrout. Correct within 30 days.
Flooring underneath the mats at the cook's lline, is very worn so that concrete is exposed and surface is rough. Flooring edges are ragged making it difficult to maintain clean. Repair flooring in this section of the facility within 90 days.
Beer cooler: screen coming loose from the ceiling. Reattach screen. Correct within 7 days.
Metal flashing located bottom left of walk in refrigerator door is deteriorated. REPEAT VIOLATION. Correct within 30 days. CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1, 114271, 114272)
