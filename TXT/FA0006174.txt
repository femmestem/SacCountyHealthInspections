# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser at the kitchen hand sink was inoperable. Corrected; dispenser was repaired during inspection. Note: Per employee, loose lid on dispenser caused malfunction. Dispenser replacement is recommended.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2) For more information on this violation please watch the following Food Safety video:

# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: One employee's California food handler card is expired. Renew within 7 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Food residue is observed on plastic food containers stored on the clean dish storage rack. Corrected; food containers were placed back in warewash sink for cleaning during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Old food debris/residue build-up is observed on floors behind the drive-thru soda machine and behind food storage rack in walk-in freezer. Clean and maintain within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
