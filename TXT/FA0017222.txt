OBSERVATIONS: Several bags of bread stored on floor of walk-in freezer. Store all food 6 inches off floor. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility.
