OBSERVATIONS: Old grease build-up is observed on a metal grate stored on the clean dish storage rack; grate is used for food storage. Corrected; grate was placed in warewash sink for cleaning during inspection. (REPEAT VIOLATION)

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred.
