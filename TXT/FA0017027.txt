# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: Manager's Food Safety Certificate is not available. Correct within 60 days and maintain on-site.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 4 EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE
OBSERVATIONS: Open employee beverage stored on shelf with dry goods. Provide closed containers and stored below/away. Correct now.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Perimeter flooring in walk in cooler/walk in freezer observed with old food residue like accumulation. Correct within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Damaged cove base paneling below walk in freezer door. Correct within 30 days. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION
Door does not fit flush against door frame on reach in cooler nearest swinging doors. Temperatures measured in compliant range. Repair immediately. 3RD REPEAT VIOLATION.
Large juicer is non functional. Correct within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
