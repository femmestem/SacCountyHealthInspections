# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: No soap and paper towels available at handwash sink in mens restroom. Soap and roll of paper towels were provided.
1 of 2 handwash sinks in kitchen was obstructed with sheet tray. Keep all handwash sinks accessible and stocked at all times. 2nd NOTICE.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2) For more information on this violation please watch the following Food Safety video:
English Cantonese Spanish Vietnamese
# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Measured cheese, ham and hot dogs at 45 F in walk-in cooler. Raw pork and raw chicken in buckets filled with water measured at 43 F. Ambient air temperature of unit was 45 F to 46 F. 2nd NOTICE.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 30b FOOD CONTAINERS LABELED
OBSERVATIONS: Facility packaged peanuts, dry bread and pastry available for self service lack labels. Products available for self service must be labeled with the following information; facility name and address, product name, ingredients, weight. Correct within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Containers holding food or food ingredients that are removed from their original packaging shall be identified with the common name of the food. (114051)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: The following areas found unclean; metro racks in dough machine room noted with old food debris, handle to 1-door freezer and walk-in cooler were greasy, floor sink under prep sink has debris accumulation. Clean within 24 hours and maintain.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Grill and propane tanks found in back room near rear exit. Remove from facility.
Formulat 3X spray bottle used to store vegetable oil. Spray bottle made from recycled materials. Provide food grade spray bottle within 7 days.

# 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: Probe thermometer not available. Provide probe thermometer that reads from 0 F to 220 F within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
# 42 GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL
OBSERVATIONS: Lids to community shared dumpster found open. Keep closed.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6, 114245.7, 114257)
