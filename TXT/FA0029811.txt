# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Open bags of cappuccino mix are stored in cabinet below the customer self-service beverage counter. Transfer food into container with tight fitting lid and label within 3 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e)) # 30a FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION OBSERVATIONS: Box of chips is stored directly on the floor near office. In addition, a box of hamburger buns is on the floor inside the walk-in freezer. Correct within 24 hours. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging. Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b)) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN OBSERVATIONS: Blue colored debris is observed inside the 2-door reach-in cooler. Clean today.

# 36 EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Probe thermometer is stored in plastic container along with 2 hair nets; employee was unsure if hair nets were used. A separate storage container was available for hair nets. Corrected; probe thermometer was cleaned and sanitized by employee during inspection.

Boxes of food saran wrap and aluminum foil are stored directly on the floor below the food prep table. Correct today.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled, displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented.
