# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Major Empty paper towel dispenser for handwash facility located in hallway prep room. Keep dispenser filled at all times. Corrected during inspection.
Minor Missing commercial soap and paper towel dispenser for handwash sink in bar. Bulk bottle of soap for 3-compartment sink locate near handwash sink and roll of paper towels locate in closet near handwash sink. Obtain dispensers within 7 days and keep stocked.
Clean equipment and tableware located on both sides of handwash sink in bar. Need to install splash guard with 6 inches in height within 7 days or relocated all clean equipment and tableware (12 inch clearance) immediately.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2) For more information on this violation please watch the following Food Safety video:

# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of food safety certificate. Obtain certificate within 30 days and keep on-site.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: One live German cockroach located on prep table next to coffee brewer in hallway prep room. Employee killed insect during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: Small square of carpet located in bar. Remove immediately. Surface must be smooth, nonabsorbent and cleanable.
Deteriorated (peeling and missing) cove base flooring in hallway prep room. Repair within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1, 114271, 114272)

# 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Missing handwash sign for restroom on 2nd floor. Provided during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
# 47b PLACARD POSTED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Placard was not posted and employee does not know where placard is located after painted walls.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Upon completion of a routine inspection or re-inspection, the Health Officer shall post at every food establishment the appropriate placard, as determined by the Health Officer. The placard shall be posted so as to be clearly visible to the general public and to patrons entering the food establishment. (Sacramento County Code 6.04.021)
# 47c INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY
OBSERVATIONS: Inspection report unavailable upon request. Keep most current inspection report available at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
