# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Pink slime inside of lip of dispenser shoot of ice machine. Wash, rinse, sanitize and air dry within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Unclean flooring beneath shelving holding unused cleaned utensils and pans at back of facility. Clean within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Low battery inside of paper towel dispenser unit at hand wash sink next to office. Replace within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
