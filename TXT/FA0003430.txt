# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: No food safety certificate. Correct in 29 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 11 REHEATING PROCEDURES FOR HOT HOLDING
OBSERVATIONS: Observed rice at 97F in steam table. Item was not reheated to above 165F before being placed into the steam table. Item was placed on to flat grill to reheat to above 165F during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods that are cooked, cooled, and subsequently reheated for hot holding or service, shall be heated to a temperature of 165F for 15 seconds. Ready-to-eat food taken from a commercailly processed hermetically sealed container, or from an intact package from a food processing plant shall be heated to a temperature of 135F for hot holding. (114016)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Steam table is not functioning well. Goes on and off and does keep food hot. Owner correct heating element. An electrician was called during inspection and will be out today to fix unit.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
