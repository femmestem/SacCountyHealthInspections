# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: The following items were out of temperature: (REPEAT VIOLATION) -Whole fried chickens (68F) on a pan on the speed rack at room temperature next to the fryer. Per owner, items were not made today and placed out at room temperature today in anticipation for future orders. No pending orders for crispy fried chicken were observed. Owner stated that since chickens will be placed in the fryer again upon order, they are left out at room temperature. Discontinue practice immediately. Corrected; items were voluntarily discarded. Education provided. -Pooled eggs (60F) in the prep cooler. Observed employees place bowl of pooled eggs from the prep cooler top into the prep cooler immediately upon reinspection arrival. Corrected; eggs remained in the prep cooler. -Bag of partial-used bean sprouts (55F) in the prep cooler. Corrected; bean sprouts remained in the prep cooler. -Tub of prefried chow mein noodles (60F) in the 1-door refrigerator. Item was not made today, per cook. Corrected; noodles remained in the refrigerator.

# 5a HANDWASHING PROCEDURES

OBSERVATIONS: One of the employees at the cooks line who was chewing on a toothpick and picking teeth with it while cooking with a wok, rinsed hands at the prep sink when asked to wash hands before proceeding to other tasks. Corrected; hands were washed at the hand sink. Education provided.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees are required to wash their hands in the following situations: before engaging in food preparation, after touching bare skin, after using the toilet room, after coughing or sneezing, after eating, drinking, or using tobacco, after handling soiled equipment or utensils, as often as necessary during food preparation to prevent cross-contamination, when switching from working with raw to ready to eat foods, before donning gloves, before dispensing or serving food, after engaging in other activities that may contaminate hands. (113952, 113953.3, 113953.4)
