# 11 REHEATING PROCEDURES FOR HOT HOLDING
OBSERVATIONS: Pasta at 118F in the hot holding box. Item was dipped in 180F water but did not get to the minimum 135F required for previously cooked items that came from a hermetically sealed container. Corrected. Item was placed into the oven to reheat to a minimum of 135F.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods that are cooked, cooled, and subsequently reheated for hot holding or service, shall be heated to a temperature of 165F for 15 seconds. Ready-to-eat food taken from a commercailly processed hermetically sealed container, or from an intact package from a food processing plant shall be heated to a temperature of 135F for hot holding.
