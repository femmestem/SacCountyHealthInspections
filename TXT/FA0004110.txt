# 34 WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: LACK OF SANITIZER TEST STRIPS. PROVIDE
CORRECT BY 8-23-15
CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used. Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125) # 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: -LOWER WALL NEAR OFFICE DOOR IN BACK STORAGE AREA OBSERVED DETERIORATED. REPAIR
-OPEN HOLES OBSERVED ON CEILING IN LIQUOR STORAGE ROOM. REPAIR
CORRECT BY 9-20-15
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1, 114271, 114272)
