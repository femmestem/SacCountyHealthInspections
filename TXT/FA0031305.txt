# 8 TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL
OBSERVATIONS: Time log is not maintained for cooked egg and cut fruit w/melons when using Time as a Public Health Control. These food items observed on customer self-service line were voluntarily discarded by food service employee during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000) # 1a DEMONSTRATION OF KNOWLEDGE
OBSERVATIONS: Food service employee did not know how to calibrate probe thermometer and food final reheating temperature. Corrected; education provided during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall have adequate knowledge of food safety as it relates to their assigned duties. (113947)
# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: California food handler card for 1 food service employee could not be located during inspection. Locate or obtain food handler card within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Minimal amount of black residue/algae observed in corner of janitorial basin in janitorial area located behind front desk. Remove all residue. Correct within 7 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
