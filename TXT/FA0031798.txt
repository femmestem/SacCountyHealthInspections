# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: 2nd REPEAT VIOLATION Lack paper towels at handsink near 3 compartment sink. Also, lack hand soap at hand sink in restroom. Corrected. Education provided.

MAJOR

MINOR VIOLATION Bar soap observed at handsink, near 3 compartment sink. Correct immediately.

Lack pump style dispenser for hand soap for hand sink near 3 compartment sink. Correct within 24 hours.
CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 4 EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE
OBSERVATIONS: Open personal beverage observed in ice cream chest freezer above sealed ready-to-eat ice cream. Corrected. NOTE: no contamination observed.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))
# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION Heavy yellow slime accumulation on white plastic baffle plate inside of ice machine. Correct and maintain within 24 hours.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Heavy black accumulation on light switches throughout facility. Correct and maintain today.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser for hand wash station, near 3 compartment sink, in disrepair. Correct within 7 days. NOTE: dispenser does not securely hold paper towels.
Hand soap dispenser at restroom hand sink in disrepair. Correct within 7 days.
