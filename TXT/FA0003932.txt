# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Shredded cheese stored on ice at food prep line measured at 57F. Insert container inside ice (bring ice level to food line) to keep item at/below 41F. Correct immediately. REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED

OBSERVATIONS: Self service area beverage dispenser nozzle accumulated with slime (gross). Remove nozzle, wash, rinse, and sanitize inside 3 compartment sink. Correct today.

Metal panel inside ice machine accumulated with pink like mold. Remove all ice, wash, rinse, and sanitize within one day. REPEAT VIOLATION.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: Hot water temperature at 3 compartment sink faucet measured at 114F. Provide at least 120F/ above. Correct within one day. REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Unclean floors between ice maker and walls. Clean within 3 days.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 47c INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY
OBSERVATIONS: Previous inspection report not available for review. Keep it inside facility at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
