# 8 TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL
OBSERVATIONS: Quiche (80F) by the cash register is to be discarded within 4 hours from the time it is removed from the oven. However, there was not documentation available to verify when food item was removed from the oven. Any food item requiring refrigeration/constant heat (at least 135F) must be time marked of the date, time removed from temperature control and time to discard. Implement immediately. Staff was to discard item at 1:30pm.
CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Domestic grade food processor (Cuisinart) and crock pot (Rival) observed in the kitchen. All equipment must be commercial grade and bear an NSF or equivalent decal. Provide signage specifying it for "Personal Use Only" or remove equipment from the premises within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
