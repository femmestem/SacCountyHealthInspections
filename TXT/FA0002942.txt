# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Ground meat 114F-129F, pulled chicken 128F-132F, and pulled beef 127F-130F stored at NEMCO warmer. Maintain hot food at/above 135F at all times. Corrected by reheating above 165F.

Minor Violations: - Top layer of shredded cheeses stored in a counter top cooler between 44F-47F next to flat grill. Employee removed extra cheese during inspection. - Diced tomatoes 50F, shredded cheese 47F at cold top cooler. Raw beef 45F, chile relleno 47F, taquito 47F, carnitas 45F, raw beef patty 44F stored at reach-in cooler. Ambient temperature was 45F. Employee lowered thermostat of this cooler to colder setting and temperature dropped to 43F within about 20 minutes. Person in charge will be checking temperature of this unit again within 30 minutes to ensure it drops to 41F or below. If not, repair order will be placed and food relocated to another cooler. - Ground beef 43F stored inside walk-in cooler. Corrected. Maintain cold food at/below 41F at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED

OBSERVATIONS: Slight black and pink slime build up on panel inside ice machine. Empty all ice, wash, rinse, sanitize and air dry within 48 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Raw sausage stored above potato wedges inside True freezer. Store raw food below ready-to-eat or less cooking food item. Correct today. Proper food storage sticker was provided. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))
# 34 WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: Employees were unable to locate QA test strips. Locate or provide within 7 days.

# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Cardboard boxes reused to line shelf above storage bin with beans and rice. Cease practice immediately. All surfaces must be smooth and easily washable at all times. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Observed sanitizer bucket with cloth towels set up with below than 150ppm quat ammonium near drive-thru window. Maintain sanitizer at least 200 ppm quat ammonium at all times. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
# 41 PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION
OBSERVATIONS: Drain pipe from front soda dispenser machine nesting inside floor sink. Provide at least 1 inch air gap within 7 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: - Holes/gap/openings observed in multiple areas throughout kitchen especially near ice machine, prep table, warewash sink, soda syrup boxes (back area). - Loose FRP wall near ceiling in the corner behind walk-in cooler and soda syrup boxes. - Missing coving behind cooking equipment (flat grill, counter top warmer and cooler unit). Raw food observed to be exposed behind these units. Seal all holes/gaps with approved material. Provide required coving. Correct within 30 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

- Loose outlet was found near chemical storage area. Secure within 7 days.
