# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of handwashing station set up in bar area. Soap and paper towels provided to basin of three compartment sink. Corrected.
NOTE: Designate permanent sink for handwashing in bar. Sticker provided. CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2) For more information on this violation please watch the following Food Safety video:
English Cantonese Spanish Vietnamese
# 1a DEMONSTRATION OF KNOWLEDGE
OBSERVATIONS: Employee unaware of how to properly test sanitizer residual. Education provided at the time of inspection. Corrected.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall have adequate knowledge of food safety as it relates to their assigned duties. (113947)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Slime accumulation in pipes draining sink basin at bar. Clean and sanitize within 7 days.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 34 WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: Chlorine sanitizer test strips unavailable upon request. Provide within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used. Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Refrigerator beneath bar in corner (containing milk products for customers) is not commercial grade. Remove or replace with commercial grade equipment within 30 days.
Household microwave and household crock pots observed in facility. Remove within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 44d EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS
OBSERVATIONS: Employee beverages, food and miscellaneous items commingled with beverages for customers. Provide a designated employee only area within 14 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)
# 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of hand washing sign in mens restroom. Provided at the time of inspection. Corrected.
Most recent health permit not visible in facility (most recent expired in 2011) provide updated permit posted within 15 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
# 49 PERMIT REQUIREMENTS
OBSERVATIONS: Facility contains smoker, BBQ, condiments and a sign advertising "clam chowder" cookoff. Facility is not permitted for these activities. Remove from facility or contact plan review at 916-874-6010 for requirements on upgrading permit for activities. Correct within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall not be open for business without a valid permit. Permit shall be posted in a conspicuous place within the food facility. (114067(b, c), 114381, 114387)
