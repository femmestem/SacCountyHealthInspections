# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Measured previously frozen, uncooked orange chicken (54f) at improper holding temperature inside of fryer basket. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHFs) at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, as orange chicken was placed inside of fryer to be fully cooked.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety manager certificate not posted inside of food facility. Correct within 24 hours.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Observed soda syrup spilt on shelf inside cabinet located below dine-in soda dispenser unit. Clean within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
