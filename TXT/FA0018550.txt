Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. # 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Ice machine observed with black/brown residues on deflector plate. Empty, wash, rinse, sanitize & air dry prior to refilling. See also #41 & #48

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Fruit flies observed in bar area. No direct contamination observed (all containers sealed). Eliminate within 7 days.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
# 41 PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION
OBSERVATIONS: Newly installed ice machine is draining to open floor drain. Correct to floor sink below metro shelving with 1 inch air gap between end of plumbing & top of floor sink within 7 days. Provide cover for floor drain to prevent American cockroach entrance within 2 days.
CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
# 48 PLAN REVIEW CONSTRUCTION AND REMODELING
OBSERVATIONS: Ice machine installed without EMD approved. Plan review done on-site by Colleen Maitoza. Any addition, subtraction or changing of equipment must be approved by EMD prior to work being done.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: A person proposing to build or remodel a food facility shall submit plans for approval before starting any new construction or remodeling of any facility for use as a retail food facility. (114380) OVERALL INSPECTION COMMENTS
