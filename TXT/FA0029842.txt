Damaged caulking observed between hand sink and wall in men's restroom. Correct within 7 days. 2ND.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation.
