# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: MAJOR VIOLATION -Observed baked chicken at 120F-144F stored in the warm display. Per operator, chicken was cooked approximately 20-30 minutes ago. Chicken was recooked to 165F then relocated to hot holding during inspection.

MINOR VIOLATION -All soup stored on the lowest and longest extending section of the soup self service stations observed at 44F-48F. Soups were relocated into the walkin refrigerator during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 34 WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: -Operator lacks quaternary ammonium test strips. Provide within 7 days.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used. Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)
# 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: -The following units lack monitoring thermometers: both lowboy refrigerators opposite the cold top, the hot and cold display units. Correct within 4 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
