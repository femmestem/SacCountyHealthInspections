# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Large bucket of raw partially frozen chicken observed inside 56F standing water. Ham inside reach in cooler measured at 53F and shredded cheese inside cold top at 55F. Reach in cooler ambient is at 46F. - Raw chicken was relocated to walk in cooler at the time of inspection. - Ham and shredded cheese that are stored inside reach in cooler & cold top were stored in ice.
Contact your service provider immediately and repair reach in cooler within 24 hours.
MINOR VIOLATION: Ground beef inside two door refrigerator, meat, chicken, and taco meat measured at 48F. Ambient temperature was at 51F and remeasured after 20 minutes to 47F. Employee provided ice and stored in ice at the time of inspection. The unit must be repaired within one day. Reinspection may be conducted to follow up compliance. Fax evidence of repairs within 72 hours to avoid reinspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

MAJOR

# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Hand wash sink inside women's restroom does not drain. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: About four flies observed inside kitchen area and several others inside dining area. Remove flies immediately from prep area and install approved fly control devices within 14 days. Fly control devices must be installed away from food storage/ prep areas.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
# 26 THAWING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: Improper thawing observed. Large bucket of frozen chicken was thawing inside 56F standing water. Frozen foods must be properly thawed using one of approved methods (inside refrigeration unit, immersed under cold running water, microwave, or grill/stove during cooking process). Correct immediately.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: Monitoring thermometer not available inside reach in cooler at prep line. Provide within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Wiping towels sanitizing solution measured at 0 ppm chlorine sanitizer. Provide chemical immediately. Corrected.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: Observed hole on the wall beneath hand wash sink. Plug/ repair within 7 days.
Cove bases at the entrance of dry storage room by 3 compartment sink are deteriorated. Repair within 30 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1, 114271, 114272)
