# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Ceilings above fan guards inside walk in cooler accumulated with mold. Clean and sanitize within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: One wiping towels sanitizing solution bucket measured at 0 ppm sanitizer. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium.
