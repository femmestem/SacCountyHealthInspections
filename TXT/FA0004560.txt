# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: 1 employee food handler card expired. Correct within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Residue and small quantities of debris on shelves in cabinet, some difficult to clean surfaces/floor areas and on exterior surfaces of icemaker. Remove residue and debris within 7 days. Protect ice from contamination when servicing.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

OBSERVATIONS: convection oven used for pork roast. No hood above convection oven. Discontinue use of convection of for food items that emit grease laden vapor.
