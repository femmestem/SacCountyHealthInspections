# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Cream cheese spread 46F inside cold drawer of sandwich prep unit and turkey sausage (with label "expired on 11-22-15") 43F-44F inside cold drawer of sandwich prep that is located across from salad prep cooler. Maintain all PHFs at/belolw 41F at all times. Manager voluntarily discarded turkey sausage during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of required hot water at hand washing stations inside both restrooms. Both restrooms have sensor operated faucets. Hot water from non readily adjustable faucets shall be between 100F-108F. Correct within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 30a FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Found box with paper bags for storing bread stored on the floor in dry storage room. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging. Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b))
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: - Black mold residue build up inside floor sink beneath soda dispenser at customer self-service area. - Trash build up on the floor beneath soda syrup boxes and CO2 cylinder. - Dust build up on vent above rethermalizer. - Ice build up on condenser unit inside walk-in freezer. - Chemical spillage observed on floor beneath dish machine. Clean and maintain within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 41 PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION
OBSERVATIONS: Observed water leak beneath 3-comp sink (middle compartment). Investigate leak and repair within 7 days. Place catch bucket to collect leaking water.

# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: - Found gap around plumbing beneath front pastry handwash station. - Broken cove base tiles, missing/chipped cove base tiles, and missing grout between floor tiles observed in bakery and food prep area. - Found hole/gap on the floor around plumbing (old restroom plumbing). CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches.
