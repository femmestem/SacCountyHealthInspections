# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Cold holding display case for sandwiches and burritoes not working properly. Burritos (bean and cheese, beef, beans and red chile, beef beans and green chile) at 51-55F, Burgers (beef, bbq pork rib) at 60F, hot pockets at 55F, sandwiches (pastrami and cheese, tuna salad, egg salad, chicken salad, turkey and cheese, ham and cheese, mega turkey and cheese) at 52-55F. Removed from sale to return for credit.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

MAJOR

# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Ice machine not clean (Black moldy build up inside). Clean within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141) # 30b FOOD CONTAINERS LABELED
OBSERVATIONS: Ice bags lacking labels. Provide approved labels on ice bags within 3 days.
<REPEAT VIOLATION>
CODE DESCRIPTION: Containers holding food or food ingredients that are removed from their original packaging shall be identified with the common name of the food. (114051) # 41 PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION
OBSERVATIONS: Faucet at mop sink lacking anti siphon valve. Provide within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
