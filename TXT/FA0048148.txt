# 42 GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL
OBSERVATIONS: 1. Large quantity of refuse in bags stacked in dumpster enclosure behind dumpster. Dispose in dumpster. Correct within 1 day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish.
