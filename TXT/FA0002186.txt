# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Observed the following rat droppings in the back storage room: <<3RD REPEAT VIOLATION>> -approximately 5 droppings on floor in the corner of back storage room furthest from kitchen door. -approximately 20-30 droppings on floor around CO2 tanks and along wall below storage racks.
Clean all droppings immediately. Provide pest control immediately and save all pest control documentation for review. A reinspection will be conducted within 24-48 hours to ensure compliance. If any droppings are observed at the time of reinspection, additional reinspections will be conducted until no activity is observed.
Back storage room has metal flashing on wall next to back entrance with gaps at the bottom and sides. Remove metal flashing and repair any holes that may be in the wall behind that can give access to vermin.
Observed gap between wall near CO2 tanks and electrical room. There appears to be a hole/gap outside of building that leads under exterior metal flashing. Seal gap within 7 days with material that is smooth, durable and easily cleanable.
NOTE: There were no rat droppings observed within the rest of facility or within the food prep area.
Storage room is not permitted to store any food or food related items that cannot be washed and sanitized. All boxes of togo supplies that are inside sealed manufacturer's containers must be removed from storage area and placed inside restaurant facility before close of business today.

Ice machine door is broken and hanging by one side. Repair/replace within 28 days. <<2ND REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Send proof of correction to inspector via fax or email (Attn: S. LePage, 916-875-8513 or lepages@saccounty.net). Failure to comply will result in a reinspection charged at $268 per inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. # 41 PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION
OBSERVATIONS: Front server station handwashing sink is non-functional. According to manager, this is due to floor sink being non-functional. Station is not in use at this time. Repair floor sink and handwashing sink within 28 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
# 44e VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION
OBSERVATIONS: Door sweep between kitchen and back storage room that is not vermin proof is damaged. Replace within 24 hours. NOTE: Door sweep will most likely be continually damaged due to floor being uneven. Once back storage room is vermin proof, this door sweep will no longer be necessary. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Back entrance still does not have a self-closing device. Provide within 5 days. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Missing vent cover in wall above back kitchen entrance from back storage room with rat activity. Electrical conduit lines are running close to opening, making it possible for rats to gain entry to kitchen ceiling. Replace vent cover immediately.
Front door does not completely self close. Door must be pulled or pushed to close completely. Repair within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)
