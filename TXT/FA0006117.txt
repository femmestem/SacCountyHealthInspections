# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Grilled pork skewers in the warmer measured at 96F-120F. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION. Corrected; items were within 4 hours and were reheated on the grill.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

MAJOR

# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: Food handler cards for most employees are not available. 2nd REPEAT VIOLATION. Correct within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 4 EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE
OBSERVATIONS: An open personal beverage was above onions and saran wrap on the metro shelf across from the 2-door refrigerator. Corrected.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))
# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: A hatched ootheca shell was on the upper edge of the cove base tile behind the prep cooler. Corrected; shell was removed and discarded. Note: No other cockroach activity was observed. Last pest control service was on 7/22/15.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Foods in the 2-door freezer were not covered. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Some grease accumulation is on the floor and back flashing behind the grill. Correct within 1 week.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 43 RESTROOMS MAINTAINED, SUPPLIED, AND ACCESSIBLE
OBSERVATIONS: Toilet paper is placed on top of empty dispenser in the women's restroom. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Toilet facilities shall be maintained and kept clean. Toilet rooms shall be separated by a well-fitting self-closing door. Toilet tissue shall be provided in a permanently installed dispenser at each toilet. The number of toilet facilities shall be in accordance with local building and plumbing ordinances. Toilet facilities shall be provided for patrons: in establishments with more than 20,000 sq. ft.; establishments offering on-site consumption of food. (114250, 114250.1, 114276)
# 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Handwash signs are missing in the men's and women's restrooms. Correct immediately. Stickers provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
# 48 PLAN REVIEW CONSTRUCTION AND REMODELING
OBSERVATIONS: Unapproved hood was installed without Plan Review or City Building Department approval. REPEAT VIOLATION. Hood was not in use at time of reinspection. Discontinue use until approved. Contact and obtain approval from the City Building Department. In addition, contact EMD Plan Review at 874-6010 and submit plans immediately. If use of the unapproved hood is observed during future inspections, an automatic compliance conference with a fee of $402 will be required.
Note: Per manager, contractor has completed blueprint plans (observed electronic copy on phone). They are waiting for plans/air balance report from Fire Protection Services and anticipate completion this weekend in order for the contractor to submit both the blueprint and fire plans together to the City Building Department. Spoke to the City Building Department inspector who was in contact with the manager as of yesterday. Complete removal of the unapproved hood may be required in the near future if facility does not show ongoing effort to make corrections and comply with Building and Health and Safety codes.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A person proposing to build or remodel a food facility shall submit plans for approval before starting any new construction or remodeling of any facility for use as a retail food facility. (114380)
