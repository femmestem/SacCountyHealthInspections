# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION The following items were out of proper holding temperature: *Bean sprouts, on turned off steam table, measured 53F- 57F. Per employee they were placed there one hour ago. Corrected by adding ice.

*Due to repeat violations a billable reinspection may be required.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 9 COOLING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION Containers of cooked pork and chicken improperly cooling in deep covered contaniers. Correct immediately. NOTE: these items measured 60F-70F 1.5 hours into cooling process. Ensure these items reach at least 41F in the next 4.5 hours. Handout provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be rapidly cooled from 135F to 70F within 2 hours, and then from 70F to 41F within 4 hours. Cooling shall be by one or more of the following methods: in shallow containers, separating food into smaller portions, adding ice as an ingredient, using an ice bath, stirring frequently, using rapid cooling equipment, or using containers that facilitate heat transfer. (114002, 114002.1)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Heavy soil in, around, and surrounding cooks line: *exhaust hood *inside deep fryer *floor below cooking equipment (2ND REPEAT VIOLATION) *sides of deep fryer *copper pipes below steam table *hinges on refrigeration units doors *below grill *ceiling above cook's line *floor below water heater Correct within 3 days and maintain.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer solution, for cloth buckets in kitchen, measured 10 ppm Cl. Correct immediately.
