#9 COOLING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: Observed cook place cooked bun (rice noodles) inside of plastic container and place lid on top for use later. After intervening, the cook was provided the education on how to properly cool the rice noodles. Corrected on site.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be rapidly cooled from 135F to 70F within 2 hours, and then from 70F to 4IF within 4 hours. Cooling shall be by one or more of the following methods: in shallow containers, separating food into smaller portions, adding ice as an ingredient, using an ice bath, stirring frequently, using rapid cooling equipment, or using containers that facilitate heat ti-ansfer. (114002, 114002.1)
