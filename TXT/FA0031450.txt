# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Half & half inside dispenser measured 61F. According to manager, item cannot be relocated to refrigeration until approximately 12 pm when an employee starts work that knows how to properly disconnect it from dispenser. An out of order sign has been placed on dispenser and it is not available for use. Half & half must be discarded and may not be sold or used for customer consumption. Correct as discussed.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towels out of dispenser at front handwashing sink. Per manager, they cannot keep it stocked because the customers will take them all. A roll of paper towels is present on the counter top. Relocate dispenser to inside cabinet under hand sink or one nearby. Correct within 3 days. <<2ND REPEAT VIOLATION>>
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Numerous house flies observed within facility. Eliminate immediately. Recommend installing an air curtain above doors or UV fly lights to help control problem.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Floor sink for walk-in cooler condensate drain is unclean. Clean within 3 days. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

OBSERVATIONS: Coffee creamer dispenser tubes are not cut at a 45 degree angle. Correct to allow creamer to fully dispense without clogging and accumulating product within 3 days.

# 44e VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION
OBSERVATIONS: Self closing device at back door is broken. Repair within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)
