# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Sliced swiss cheese at 55 F, sliced pepper jack at 58 F, sliced cheddar cheese at 52 F in prep line. Cheese had been in prep line no more than 2 hours. Foot long hot dogs at 51 F in cold drawer. Hot dogs had been in place no more than 1.5 hours.
MINOR VIOLATION Sliced tomatoes at 45 in prep line. Small hot dogs at 50 F and shredded cheese at 49 in prep line cold drawers. Grilled onions at 43 F, sliced cheeses at 44 F, chicken breasts at 44 F in cold drawer under grill.
Hot dogs were relocated to walk in refrigerator. Lids were placed on prep line and cold drawers were closed fully. Monitor to ensure 41 F is reached. Corrected.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: Food handler cards not available for employees. Provide proof of food handler cards on site at all times. Correct - 7 days. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION. Note: Failure to comply with this requirement at future inspection may result in a reinspection at cost or other enforcement action.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Soap in squeeze bottle at front hand sink nearest reach in freezer and back hand sink. Provide soap in approved dispensers. Correct - 24 hours. Soap dispenser at back hand sink was cleaned out and refilled, and functioning at end of inspeciton.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Debris on floors under prep refrigerator. Correct - 24 hours.
Debris in 3 compartment sink. Correct - 24 hours.
Build up on walk in refrigerator shelving, fan covers and ceiling near condenser. Correct - 3 days.
