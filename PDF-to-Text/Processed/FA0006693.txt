# 32a FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING
OBSERVATIONS: Tofu and various spices are portioned out into smaller portions for customers to buy. Provide a label with name, city, state and zipcode of the facility, manufacturer or distributor, common name of food and net weight. Correct within 7 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food offered for human consumption shall be honestly presented in a way that does not mislead or misinform the consumer. # 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Bleach sanitizer inside bucket is at 50 ppm. Keep at 100 ppm at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
