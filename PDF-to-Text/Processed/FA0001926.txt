# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: The following potentially hazardous food items were stored at improper holding temperature (cold top/reach in combo unit):
Stored in cold top unit: - one metal insert of cut tomatoes (44F) - one metal insert of cooked onion (52F) - tuna mixture (49F)
Stored inside reach in cooler: - one metal tray of sliced cheese (45F) - sliced ham (46F-50F) - one metal insert of diced ham (55F) - shredded cheese (48F) - prepackaged sliced monerey jack cheese (56F) - 1/4 lbs. raw chicken (50F) - one bag of corned beef (51F) - boiled eggs (49F)

MAJOR

Maintain all potentially hazardous food at/below 41F for cold food and at/above 135F for hot food. Keep cold top lids closed when not in use. Keep inserts only ¾ full to help maintain temperature.
Note: the ambient temperature of this cold top/reach in combo unit was measured at 46F-48F at arrival of inspection. All noted items (except the items that were measured at 51F and above) were relocated to 1 door upright cooler at cook's line. Manager turned thermostat to colder setting during inspection and will monitor to ensure ambient temperature drops to 41F.

# 36 EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Clean utensils stored with mouth parts up at ware wash area. All food equipment must be protected from contamination. Store utensils with mouth parts down to prevent from contamination. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled, displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3, 114185.4, 114185.5)
