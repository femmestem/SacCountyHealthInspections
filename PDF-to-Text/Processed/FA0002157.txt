# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Deep fried chicken on counter top measured at 61F - 66F. Owner stated that food item was done cooking about an hour ago. Owner transfer food item to refrigerator. Corrected at time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed server washing hands without soap. Education provided. Corrected at time of inspection.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: Maximum temperature of hot water at warewash sink was measured at 116F. Provide hot water at warewash sink at 120 F minimum at all times. Correct today.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: 1. Observed old food debris accumulated on shelves inside kitchen next to deep fryer. Clean today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: 1. Observed domestic GE upright freezer and chest freezer. Provide commercial equipment in 6 months.
2. Observed cardboard used inside to line containers and cardboard boxes used to store chicken. Discontinue use of cardboard boxes to store food items. Corrected at time of inspection.
