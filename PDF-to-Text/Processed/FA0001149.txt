# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Lack pump style dispenser for hand soap in men's restroom. Correct within 24 hours.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Dust observed on the following areas: *fan guards for Red Bull cooler and upright cooler *shelves throughout sales floor Correct and maintain today.
CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Cardboard used as a liner on shelves on sales floor. Corrected.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)
# 52 OTHER
OBSERVATIONS: Observed open bulk container of Red Vines and large open jar of pickles on cash register counter. NOTE: Permit for this facility only allows selling of pre-packaged food items.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: California Retail Food Code
