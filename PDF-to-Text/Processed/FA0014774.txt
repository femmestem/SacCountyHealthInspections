# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: California food handler card is not available for Bartender. Obtain within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Moderate dust build-up is observed on walls and fan guards inside the walk-in refrigerator. Clean and maintain within 7 days.
Old beverage syrup build-up is observed on floor inside the walk-in refrigerator. Clean and maintain within 7 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 34 WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS
OBSERVATIONS: Facility lacks quaternary ammonia and chlorine sanitizer test strips. Provide within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used. Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Multiple crock pots are observed in the facility; per Bartender, crock pots belong to customers. Remove unapproved equipment from facility within 3 months. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
Cinder blocks are used to prop up the ice machine and boxes of soda syrup. Remove or replace with a smooth, washable surface within 7 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
Excessive rust build-up is observed on floor of beer chest refrigerator. Clean and remove rust within 14 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)
# 36 EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: CO2 cylinders stored in back room are not secured. Secure cylinders to a rigid structure to prevent from tipping over. Correct within 7 days.
Ice scoop handle is stored in contact with ice at bar. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled, displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3, 114185.4, 114185.5)
# 47b PLACARD POSTED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: A copy of the most recent routine health inspection report could not be located during inspection. Correct immediately. (REPEAT VIOLATON)
CODE DESCRIPTION: Upon completion of a routine inspection or re-inspection, the Health Officer shall post at every food establishment the appropriate placard, as determined by the Health Officer. The placard shall be posted so as to be clearly visible to the general public and to patrons entering the food establishment. (Sacramento County Code 6.04.021)
# 49 PERMIT REQUIREMENTS
OBSERVATIONS: Hot dog cooker and multiple condiments observed in the facility. Permit requirements do not allow for hot dogs. Remove from facility or contact EMD plan review to meet the requirements to cook hot dogs within 7 days. Email proof of correction to yangt@saccounty.net. Failure to comply could result in a reinspection at $201.00. (REPEAT VIOLATION-2ND NOTICE)
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall not be open for business without a valid permit. Permit shall be posted in a conspicuous place within the food facility. (114067(b, c), 114381, 114387)
