# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Major violation:

- Measured pan of fried chicken (108f-114f) inside left side of Halo glass case. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHFs) at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, as fried chicken was reheated inside of microwave unit and left side of Halo glass case was also turned up.

- Open bag of shredded cheese (54f) on counter near sandwich cold top line. Maintain all PHFs at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, as open bag of cheese was relocated into reach in refrigeration unit below.

- Several (10) raw shelled eggs (58-61f) on counter near sandwich cold top line. Maintain all PHFs at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, eggs were allowed to be relocated into reach in refrigeration unit below.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
