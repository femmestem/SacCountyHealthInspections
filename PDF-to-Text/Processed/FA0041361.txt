# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Raw chicken walk-in floor has debris and wall behind/under drive-thru soda fountain has splatter. Correct within 2 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED OBSERVATIONS: Men's restroom lacks a handwash sign. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
