Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Slight slime like buildup observed inside floor sink under the three compartment sink. Clean and maintain within 3 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES OBSERVATIONS: Monitoring thermometer not available in open air case holding salads. Provide within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
# 44e VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION
OBSERVATIONS: Gap greater than 1/4 of an inch at bottom of back door. Daylight is visible. Provide door sweep to prevent vermin entrance. Correct within 14 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)
# 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Health permit is not clearly visible for public view. Provide within 7 days. Contact inspector if a new permit needs to be mailed.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
