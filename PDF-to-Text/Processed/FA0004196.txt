# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Bean sprouts stored in cold-top #2 measured 46F. Recommend placing a lid on pan insert to help keep cold. Correct immediately.
Cooked chicken inside walk-in cooler measured 43F. Ambient temperature of unit at the level of chicken (about 3 feet above floor) measured 43F; ambient temperature of unit at the top of storage racks measured 38F. Repair technician was on-site at the time of inspection and will lower thermostat to 35F. Ensure all potentially hazardous foods in walk-in maintain at/below 41F at all times.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Observed heavy ice build-up inside walk-in cooler. Correct within 29 days.
Gaskets torn in 3-door upright reach-in and inside 2-door cold-top reach-in next to cooking equipment. Replace within 29 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
