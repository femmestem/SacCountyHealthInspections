# 6 HANDWASHING FACILITIES
OBSERVATIONS: Major violations:
- Hand wash sink located near drive-thru was blocked by a grease handle unit. Make all hand wash sinks clear and accessible at all times. Corrected on site.
- No paper towels inside of paper towel dispenser unit at hand wash sink near drive-thru. Maintain paper towels inside of paper towel dispenser unit at all times. Corrected on site, as a roll was placed on top of unit. Third Repeat Violation
Minor violation:
- Hand wash sink near fryers had paper towel roll stored outside of paper towel dispenser unit. Maintain paper towel roll inside of unit at all times. Corrected on site, as a paper towel roll was placed on top of unit. Third Repeat Violation.
- No batteries inside of paper towel roll dispenser at hand wash sink located near fryers. Correct immediately.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. OBSERVATIONS: - Leak below three compartment sink. Repair within 5 days. Second Repeat Violation.
- Leak at spray gun of three compartment sink. Repair within 5 days. Second Repeat Violation.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or utensil handling areas. # 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: - Deteriorated wall near drive thru POS. Repair within 10 days. Third Repeat Violation.

- Missing cove tile base near drive thru POS. Repair within 10 days. Third Repeat Violation.

- Missing cove tile between dry storage shelf and mop sink. Repair within 10 days. Third Repeat Violation.

- Food debri on floor of walk in freezer unit. Clean within 24 hours. Second Repeat Violation.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches.
