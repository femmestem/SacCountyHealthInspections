# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Supper program sandwiches and meat wraps in the chest refrigerator measured at 43-45F. Ambient of unit is at 50F according to the internal monitoring thermometer. Sandwiches are delivered and stored in closed igloos in the chest refrigerator. Corrected; technician arrived onsite and lowered thermostat of unit. Ensure that foods inside the igloo are maintained at/below 41F, otherwise, it is recommended that the igloo lid be kept open for proper air flow exchange until proper food temperature is reached.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
