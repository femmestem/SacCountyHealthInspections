# 1c FOOD HANDLER CARDS
OBSERVATIONS: -Food handler card has expired for employee Sthephanie Ramos. Correct within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food handlers that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall obtain a valid Food Handler Card within 30 days after date of hire. (113948)
# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: -Observed ham, turkey and chicken deli meats at 45F-46F inside the low boy refrigerator unit. All deli meats were relocated into the true refrigerator during time of inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: -Old syrup buildup observed on the nozzles of the soda machine. Clean and maintain within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: REPEAT VIOLATION -Ambient temperature inside the low boy refrigerator unit observed at 46F. Ensure that this refrigeration unit is able to maintain all potentially hazardous foods at 41F and below before using. Correct within 7 days and provide proof of correction or use of this unit to store potentially hazardous food will not be allowed.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
