# 30a FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Bus tub of frozen chicken stored on floor below a rack in walk in cooler. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging. Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. # 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Bowl utilized as a scoop in bulk container of rice. Utilize handled scoops. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair.
