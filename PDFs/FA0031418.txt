# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: - Measured one pan of cooked chicken (127-131f) and one pan of cooked steak (124-130f) to both be at improper holding temperatures inside of Halo warmer unit. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHFs) at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, as both food items were reheated to 165f and heat dial on Halo unit was also turned up.

- Measured an over-portioned pan of cut tomatoes (53f) inside of front cold top line. Maintain all PHFs at/below 41f when under refrigeration. Corrected on site, as over-portioned amount of cut tomatoes were removed and relocated into reach in refrigeration unit below. (Note: Avoid storing any PHF's above load fill line inside of cold top pans).

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Two uncovered pans of raw white fillets inside of reach in refrigeration unit located across grill. Corrected on site.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e)) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: The following areas are in need of cleaning:
- Below dishwasher unit - Below soda syrup box station near back door
Clean within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: Lack of a probe thermometer (0-220f) available upon request. Provide within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food.
