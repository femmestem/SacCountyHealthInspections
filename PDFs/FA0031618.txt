# 32a FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING
OBSERVATIONS: Ice for sale observed in bags which lack label identifying facility's name and address. Operator showed that they have the stamp. Correct within 2 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food offered for human consumption shall be honestly presented in a way that does not mislead or misinfonn the consumer. Food packaged in a food facility shall bear a label that complies with the labeling requirements prescribed by the Shennan Food, Dmg, and Cosmetic Law which include: common name of the food, ingredients list, quantity of contents, name and address of manufacturer, nutritional infonnation as specified in 21 C.F.R. # 39 TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES
OBSERVATIONS: Monitoring thermometer in merchandiser with prepackaged sandwiches and dairy items was situated at the rear of unit and cannot be read with a flashlight (reading was 25 F). Code requires monitoring thermometers for refrigeration units to be located at the warmest parts of the units and where they are easily readable. Correct within 3 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thennometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available to the food handler. Thennometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their accuracy. A thennometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines. (114157, 114159)
# 43 RESTROOMS MAINTAINED, SUPPLIED, AND A C C E S S I B L E
OBSERVATIONS: Self-closing device was removed from the restroom door. Correct within 5 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Toilet facilities shall be maintained and kept clean. Toilet rooms shall be separated by a well-fitting self-closing door. Toilet tissue shall be provided in a permanently installed dispenser at each toilet. The number of toilet facilities shall be in accordance with local building and plumbing ordinances. Toilet facilities shall be provided for patrons: in establishments with more than 20.000 sq. ft.; establishments offering on-site consumption of food. (114250. 114250.1, 114276)
