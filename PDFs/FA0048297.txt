# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: MAJOR- Soft cheese sticks placed on paper boats for service measured at 55F. Maintain at/below 41F at all times. Discussed use of Time as a Public Health Control for potentially hazardous foods at time of inspection. Corrected by moving cheese sticks into reach in refrigerator at time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return

to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Womens restroom hand wash station lacks hot water. Provide hot water between 100-108F within 48 hours.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer concentration at wiping towel container measured at 150ppm quat. ammonia. Keep sanitizer concentration at 200ppm quat. ammonia at all times. Correct now.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
