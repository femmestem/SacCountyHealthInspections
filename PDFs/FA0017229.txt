# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: The following food items are stored out of proper holding temperature (no TPHC stickers used): -Liquid egg whites on grill prep table at 61F -Raw shell eggs on grill prep table at 48F -Liquid eggs on grill prep table at 48F
Corrected; liquid egg whites was cooked to above 165F during inspection. TPHC stickers were place on raw shell eggs and liquid eggs for a 20 minutes of maximum use time. Per employee, food items have been removed from refrigeration no more than 10 minutes. Note: Failure to fully comply with TPHC requirement may result in food destruction and major violation.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

MAJOR

# 1b FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION
OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certification could not be located for inspection. Locate and retain on-site for next inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)
# 14b WAREWASHING AND SANITIZING PROCEDURES
OBSERVATIONS: Automatic dish machine lacks adequate sanitizer residual during final rinse cycle, 0 ppm Cl measured. Correct within 24 hours.
Note: Sanitizer hopper located above dish machine unit was empty. Dish machine was not in-use at time of observation.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food-contact surfaces and multiservice utensils shall be effectively washed, rinsed, and sanitized to remove or completely loosen soils by use of manual or mechanical methods. The temperature of the washing solution shall be maintained at least 100F or at the temperature specified by the manufacturer's label. Manual sanitation shall be accomplished during the final rinse by contact with solution containing 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Mechanical sanitization shall be accomplished in the final rinse by achieving a utensil surface temperature of 160F or by contact with solution containing 50 ppm available chlorine. After cleaning and sanitizing, equipment and utensils shall be air dried. (114097, 114099.1, 114099.2, 114099.4, 114099.6, 114099.7, 114101 (b-d), 114105, 114109, 114111)
# 27 FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Open bag of sugar is stored in drive-thru area. Transfer food into a container with tight fitting lid and label within 2 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Chlorine sanitizer solution in wipe cloth bucket measured 50 ppm. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
# 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Kitchen hand sink is missing a handwashing sign. Provide within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
# 47c INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY
OBSERVATIONS: A copy of the most recent routine health inspection report could not be located for inspection. Correct immediately.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
