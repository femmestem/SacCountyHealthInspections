# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Found all potentially hazardous food items at 46F on the large cold top unit that was holding ambient air at 53F. Corrected on site temporarily via ice bath. Service scheduled at time of routine inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037) # 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: Hot water supply at 75F on sensor hand sinks. Keep at 100-108F for all sensor type hand sinks. Correct in 2 days.
NOTE: Did not check women's restroom due to occupancy. Verify all hand sinks can supply 100-108F range.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Found the white fan unclean with heavy grease accumulation located above one of the grill. Correct in 3 days.
Found the interior of the one door cold top refrigerator unclean. Clean today
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 47a SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED
OBSERVATIONS: Missing hand washing sign inside the restrooms. Provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
