# 14a FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED
OBSERVATIONS: Soda nozzles and base panels below and above nozzles observed with old soda syrup stain/accumulation. Clean and maintain within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred. Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141) # 23 VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION
OBSERVATIONS: Observed one dead mice inside cabinet underneath soda machine. Observed about 20 droppings inside the same cabinet. There is no food or food related equipment in this cabinet. This cabinet is used to hide compressor/motor for the soda machine. Remove mice, clean and sanitize the area. Correct immediately.
Observed 10 rodent droppings inside cabinet where flashlights are stored. There is no food or food related equipment in this cabinet. Remove, clean, and sanitize the area. Correct immediately.
Note: facility is serviced by Orkin once per month. Observed new/clean mouse traps and glue pads throughout facility. Most recent invoice from Orkin was sent to accounting and was not available during inspection. Please make a copy of the most recent report from Orkin and keep it on-site going forward.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5) # 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Fan guard inside 3 door upright refrigerator observed with dust accumulation. Clean and maintain within 48 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
