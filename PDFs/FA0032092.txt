# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: -Observed slight dust accumulation on the ceiling by the fan guard inside the walkin refrigerator. Clean and maintain within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257) # 44e VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION OBSERVATIONS: -Observed a one inch crack at the bottom left corner of the back door. Eliminate gap within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or utensil handling areas.
