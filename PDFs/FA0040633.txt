# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY
OBSERVATIONS: Maximum temperature of hot water at the 3 compartment sink measured at 95F. Heat setting on hot water heater was adjusted during inspection. Provide hot water, minimum 120F at all times. Hot water was measured 101F towards the end of inspection. Correct within 24 hours.
CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 30a FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION
OBSERVATIONS: Observed 1 syrup container on floor in back storage area. Corrected today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging. Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b))
# 40 WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE
OBSERVATIONS: Measured 1 sanitizer bucket at front area at less than 100 ppm chlorine. Maintain at least 100 ppm chlorine at all times. Correct today.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).
# 45 FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES
OBSERVATIONS: Observed one ceiling panel missing in back handwashing area. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1, 114271, 114272)
