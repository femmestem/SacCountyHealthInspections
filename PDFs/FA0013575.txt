# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed an over-portioned amount of portioned teriyaki chicken (54f) trays and shredded steak (53f) trays inside of front cold top line. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHFs) at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on site, as extra portioned trays of teriyaki chicken and shredded steak were relocated into walk in refrigeration unit. (Do not store any PHFs above load fill line inside of insert pans).

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21 HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Measured hot water at 109f at three compartment sink. Provide at least 120f hot water at three compartment sink. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Food debri on floor near corner of water heater. Clean within 24 hours.

MAJOR

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 35 EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED
OBSERVATIONS: Dusty fan grills inside of upright beverage refrigeration unit located in front lobby. Clean within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized.
