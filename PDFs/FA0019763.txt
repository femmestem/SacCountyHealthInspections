# 7 HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES
OBSERVATIONS: Observed half & half for customers at front area measured at 43F-45F. Keep all potentially hazardous food items at/below 41F for cold food items and at/above 135F for hot food items. Corrected by relocating the noted item to 3 door upright cooler in back. Per employee, time as a public health control (TPHC) will be used for noted food item. Note: "Time as a Public Health Control" handout provided during inspection.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
# 33 NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN
OBSERVATIONS: Ceiling vents above sandwich cold top observed with heavy dust accumulation. Clean and maintain within 14 days.
CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
# 48 PLAN REVIEW CONSTRUCTION AND REMODELING
OBSERVATIONS: 5TH REPEAT VIOLATION
Coffee roaster installed without EMD Plan Review approval. Contact EMD Plan Review at (916) 874-6010 for further compliance within 7 days.
A RE-INSPECTION MAY BE CONDUCTED TO DETERMINE COMPLIANCE. A $268 FEE MAY APPLY FOR RE-INSPECTION. CONTINUED NON-COMPLIANCE MAY RESULT IN A COMPLIANCE CONFERENCE BEING REQUIRED. A $402 FEE APPLIES TO EACH COMPLIANCE CONFERENCE.
CODE DESCRIPTION: A person proposing to build or remodel a food facility shall submit plans for approval before starting any new construction or remodeling of any facility for use as a retail food facility. (114380)
